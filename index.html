Welcome to Cosmos

Solar System Explorer & Asteroid Defense

🌍 Explore the Solar System

Navigate through a beautifully rendered model of our Solar System — all eight planets with realistic surface details, their major moons, a comet, the asteroid belt, shooting stars, and stunning nebula backgrounds. Click any planet to discover real facts.

🛡️ Asteroid Defense Game

Click the Defend button to activate the Asteroid Defense game mode! Dangerous asteroids will start hurtling toward Earth. Click them to destroy them before they hit — each impact costs a life. Survive waves of increasing difficulty, earn combo bonuses, and use your shield power-up to clear the screen!

  • Click asteroids to destroy them (bigger = more points)
  • Combo bonus — destroy asteroids in quick succession for multipliers
  • Shield — press S to activate (30-second cooldown)
  • Waves — each wave spawns more & faster asteroids
  • Lives — you start with 5. An asteroid hitting Earth costs one life

🖱️ Navigation

  • Scroll wheel — Zoom in and out
  • Click + drag on empty space — Pan the view
  • Click a planet or the Sun — View detailed information

🔭 The Solar System

Our Solar System formed approximately 4.6 billion years ago from the gravitational collapse of a molecular cloud. It contains one star (the Sun), eight planets, over 200 moons, and countless asteroids and comets.

💡 Tips

  • Zoom in close to gas giants to see their moons orbiting.
  • Watch for the comet streaking through the system with its glowing tail!
  • In Defend mode, bigger asteroids give more points but are harder to hit.
  • Use the shield strategically — save it for when you're overwhelmed!
